Is Specter really free?

Absolutely. We only have the free plan that allows you to send unlimited invoices to an unlimited number of clients, using an unlimited number of institutions.

Are my invoices/receipts safe?

Keeping your data secure is one of our top priorities — that's why we did not spare any expenses when it comes to our encrypted server systems. This industry-leading setup enables us to sync your private invoicing data to the cloud.

Does Specter work on my Mac, PC and mobile device?

Absolutely — since Specter is a web-based application you'll be able to keep track of your invoicing needs using any device with an internet connection — whether you're at home, in the office or on the go. Moreover we have a mobile app too!
